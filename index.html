    About Us

    Exon Biotechnology is under the Beyler group umbrella founded as a result of nearly 30 years of experience in various fields. Beyler's main fields of activity are Health, Informatics, Defense, Finance and Consultancy. Exon biotechnology runs numerous labs and research projects in the health sector.

    WE'RE ADVANCING THE HEALTH SECTOR THROUGH MANUFACTURING DIFERENT TEST KITS & TRANSPORT MEDIUMS.

    We are a registered company in Uganda with UIA, NDA, NEMA, KCCA, UVRI and Ministry of Health.

    We are mainly manufacturing Genetic PCR Test Kits, Transport Mediums and Rapid Tests in Turkey and Uganda. The Ministry of health through the Uganda Virus Institute has validated these Kits including herpes virus, SMA PCR , Hermorrhagic Congo Fever PCR, Respiratory Syncytial Virus(RSV) test Kits.

    EXON Genetics & Biotechnology established 2021 in Kampala, Uganda, and serves as a specialist in Laboratory Test Kits Production. EXON is under the Umbrella of BEYLER GROUP.

    BEYLER GROUP is amongst the leading companies in Turkey in terms of Healthcare and Medical Services,Defense Industry, IT and Consultancy.Visit Beyler Group.
