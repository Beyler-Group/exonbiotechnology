    About Us

    Exon Biotechnology is under the Beyler group umbrella founded as a result of nearly 30 years of experience in various fields. Beyler's main fields of activity are Health, Informatics, Defense, Finance and Consultancy. Exon biotechnology runs numerous labs and research projects in the health sector.

    WE ARE THE FIRST COMPANY TO MANUFACTURE REAL TIME PCR TEST KITS IN AFRICA.

    We are a registered company in Uganda with Uganda Investment Authority, National Drug Authority, National Environmental Management Authority, Kampala City Council Authority, Uganda Virus Research Institute and Ministry of Health.

    We're advancing Uganda's health industry through our well equiped labs, highly Qualified lab personals and more

    We are mainly producing Polymerase Chain Reaction Kits(PCR). The Ministry of health through the Uganda Virus Institute has validated these Kits including herpes virus, SMA PCR , Hermorrhagic Congo Fever PCR, Respiratory Syncytial Virus(RSV) test kits.

    Services

    We provide a wide range of approved tests in our fully equiped labs at our locations and following tests as below.

    GENETIC TESTS

    Genetic testing involves examining the DNA, the chemical database that carries instructions for your body.

    BIOCHEMISTRY TESTS

    Samples of serum, plasma and urine are analysed and levels of specific chemicals are measured.

    MICROBIOLOGICAL TESTS

    The detection, identification or enumeration of microorganisms is performed for a given material(food, drinks, clinical sample).

    HORMONE TESTS

    Detect and evaluate hormone imbalances that may be making you sick.

    HEMOGRAM AND COAGULATION TESTS

    Complete blood count, erythrocyte sedimentation rate(ESR) assessing the overall blood health.

    RAPID TESTS(ANTIGEN & ANTIBODY)

    Detects protein fragments specific to Coronavirus.
